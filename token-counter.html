What is a token?
AI models like Claude and GPT don't read words — they read tokens: chunks of characters determined by a BPE vocabulary. Common words are often one token; rare words split into several. Spaces, punctuation, and capitalisation all affect the split.

This page uses the cl100k_base tokeniser — the same encoding used by GPT-4 and Claude.
